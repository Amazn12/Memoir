HW3

My application will be using both one's visual and audio senses to track memories. Both photographs and music elicits emotions and help trigger memories and past events. Music often brings nostalgia and specific emotions that one felt during a specific time frame. The system will therefore let the user upload both one image and one audio clip everyday. The image can be a photograph that the user took that day or an image found online that reminds them of that specific day. The audio clip can either be a song that they just found that day or one that they listened to a lot during the day. With images and audio clips uploaded, the user can then review the past days by flip through each day similar to a blog or an album book. It will be like listening to the soundtrack of one's life.
